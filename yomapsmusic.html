Biography

Yo Maps is a multi-talented Zambian artist blending contemporary beats with authentic Zambian sounds Under Olios Records. He is a businessman, brand ambassador for several local and international brands, and an influential figure in the music industry. His music spans genres like Afro-pop, R&B, and contemporary Zambian music, reaching fans worldwide through Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube.
