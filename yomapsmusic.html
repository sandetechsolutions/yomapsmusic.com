Biography

Elton Mulenga, famously known as Yo Maps, is a multi-talented Zambian artist blending contemporary beats with authentic Zambian sounds. Under Olios Records, he is a musician, businessman, and brand ambassador whose music spans Afro-pop, R&B, and contemporary Zambian styles, reaching fans worldwide through major streaming platforms.
